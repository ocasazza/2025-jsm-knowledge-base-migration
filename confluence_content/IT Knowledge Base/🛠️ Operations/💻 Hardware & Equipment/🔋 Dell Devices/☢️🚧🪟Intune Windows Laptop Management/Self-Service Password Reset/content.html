Prerequisites

  • Previously registered authentication methods

  • Access to a secondary verification device/email

Reset Procedure

  1. Navigate to $PASSWORD_RESET_URL (typically https://passwordreset.microsoftonline.com )

  2. Enter your corporate email address

  3. Complete the CAPTCHA verification

  4. Select your verification method:

    • SMS verification

    • Voice call verification

    • Email verification

    • Security questions

  5. Complete the identity verification process

  6. Create a new password that adheres to complexity requirements

  7. Authenticate with your new credentials

Account Recovery Escalation

If you are unable to complete the self-service reset:

  1. Contact the helpdesk at $IT_SUPPORT_EMAIL or $IT_SUPPORT_PHONE

  2. Provide your username and employee identification

  3. Complete identity verification as per support protocol

  4. Temporary credentials will be issued within 15 minutes of request
