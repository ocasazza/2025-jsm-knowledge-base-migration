Enrolling your Apple Device to Jamf "Schrödinger"
This page provides instructions on how to set up your Schrödinger MacBook Pro or Air.
Once you have received your laptop and login information, please follow the directions below.
After turning on your laptop, a series of screens will pop up with instructions. In just a few steps, you can register and set up your Mac

If you encounter a problem enrolling your computer in Jamf, you may need to try re-enrolling. See Re-enrolling a machine with a failed Jamf install .

Get your device managed by Schrödinger


Complete the following steps to enroll your macOS device in Jamf management

  1. Power on your macOS device.

  2. Choose your country/region and click Continue

  3. Select your Wi-Fi network. You must have an internet connection to continue the setup. Wifi or Ethernet are both options that will allow the setup to proceed.

  4. After you've connected to Wi-Fi, the Remote Management screen appears. Remote management enables your organization's administrator to remotely configure your device with company-required accounts, settings, apps, and networks. Click Continue .

  5. When prompted, sign in with your Schrödinger account. After you've authenticated, your device will install a management profile. The profile configures and enables your access to our company's resources.

  6. After your device is enrolled, you have additional steps to complete the personalization of your device such as Turn on Location Service (must be enabled for services to work), Accessibility settings, Touch-ID, etc.

  7. Once the device is done with the base configuration, you will be prompted to sign into the laptop using your Okta information.

  8. Desktop will appear with default apps when basic configuration is completed.

  9. Apps can now be downloaded via Self Service.

  10. Druva InSync will install automatically. Restore data back to device if upgrading once you see Druva.

  11. SentinelOne will install automatically.

  12. Device is setup. Please raise a ticket with IT Ops for further assistance or questions.
