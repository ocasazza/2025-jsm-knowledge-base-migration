How to Install/Update Jamf Connect on your Mac:

  1. Navigate to the Self Service Application

Open Self Service on the Mac by going to Finder ‚Üí Applications ‚Üí Self Service (It will look like this application icon listed below)

2. Install Latest Jamf Connect via Self Service

Launch Self Service ‚Üí Click Browse ‚Üí Click JAMF Connect subsection.
Run JAMF Connect Install by clicking the install button on the icon listed below in Self Service (it may also be in the Featured tab in Self Service).

3. Restart Mac & Re-authenticate with Okta Credentials

Restart the Mac. Upon Restart, you will be prompted to log back into the device with your Okta credentials. The below message prompts will appear to let you know when to restart your computer.
