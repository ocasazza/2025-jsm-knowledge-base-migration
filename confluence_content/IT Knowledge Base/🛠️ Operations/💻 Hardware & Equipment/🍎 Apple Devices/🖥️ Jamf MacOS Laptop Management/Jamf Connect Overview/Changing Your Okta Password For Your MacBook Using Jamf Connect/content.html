Jamf Connect is an app that allows administrators to manage authentication by connecting a user's local macOS account to their organization's cloud identity (network account).

After installing and enrolling in Jamf Connect you will have a new menu bar icon at the top right of your MacBook. This is the Jamf Connect Menu bar, and it allows you to interface with Okta and perform Okta password changes and resets from the menu bar.


When it comes time to change your Okta password, changing it via Jamf Connect is the most seamless way to keep your MacBook and Okta passwords in sync .


Pre-requisites:

  • Your current Okta password. (If Jamf connect was already installed and set up on your device.) or current local device password.

Step-by-Step-Guide:

  1. Select the Jamf Connect Menu bar Icon.

  2. Clicking Change Password pops up an Okta window for you to log in to. Once logged in, it actually brings you to a mini window of your settings screen in Okta where you can change your password. When it comes time to change your Okta password, you can do so through the convenient Jamf Connect menu bar at the top, this is the most seamless way to keep your Macbook and Okta passwords in sync .



  3. Scroll down and enter your Current Okta password . Enter a New password in the New Password field. Confirm your New password and click Change password at the bottom of the change password dialogue box . Then select Done.

    The change password box below can be expanded by clicking the green plus icon in the upper left or dragging from the lower right-hand corner of the box.




    At this point of the process your local password and the new Okta password will be out of sync. Your local password does not automatically get updated with the new one you just set. Steps 4 through 6 will update the local password.


  4. You will then get a prompt asking you to Sign in to Jamf Connect again to complete your password change . Select OK.



  5. You will then be prompted to the Jamf connect Sign in window. Sign in using your Schrodinger Email Address and New OKTA Password.



  6. Almost done: Enter your old Okta password or your local laptop password.

    If this is the first time you are installing Jamf Connect this will be the password of your local user account, otherwise it will be the previous Okta password.



  7. Done: The Jamf Connect sign-in box should close and your Okta password should be synced to your local device.


If you change your password via any other method than described above (e.g. using the Okta website) you will have to log into your MacBook with your previous password . It may take up to an hour for Jamf Connect to notice your passwords are not in Sync, but after Jamf Connect will remind you every 15 minutes untilÂ  you synchronize your MacBook password with your Okta password.


Alternatively, you can click the Jamf Connect menu bar icon and click "Connect...". This will open an Okta login window for you to sign in and synchronize your passwords.
