This page provides instructions for installing your Schrödinger email signature. Please follow the directions below to generate and setup your new signature:

Generating Your Signature:

1.) Fill out this Signature Generator Form and click "Get Signature"

2.) Once you have submitted the form above you will see your new signature on the left and HTML markup that is needed to install your signature in some programs on the right.

3.) Choose your email client from the following list for program-specific instructions:

  • Apple Mail

  • Gmail.com

  • Outlook 2019

Apple Mail App applemail :

1.) Open Mail and click Mail --> Preferences   --> Signatures

2.) In the left-hand box, choose the account you would like to install your signature for (this will likely say Gmail for schrodinger.com emails).

3.) Click the " + " button under the middle box and give your signature any name you prefer.

4.) Fill out the Signature Generator Form above and click "Get Signature".

5.) Copy your signature from the left-hand side of the page and paste it into the right-hand box of the Signature panel you just had open in Apple Mail. Be sure when you paste it that your new signature is selected in middle box.

6.) Don't worry if the image appears as a question mark in this box. Make sure that "Always match my default font" is NOT checked and click the red " X " in the upper left-hand corner to close the Signatures panel.

7.) Verify your new signature is working by composing a new message and then selecting your new signature from the drop down list. The image should now be shown as long as you are connected to the internet.

gmail.com Setup gmailmail :

1.) Log-in to your Gmail account using a browser of your choice.

2.) Click the "gear" icon in the upper right hand corner of the screen.

3.) Select "Settings" from the drop down menu that appears.

4.) Scroll down the page until you see "Signature:"

5.) Fill out the Signature Generator Form above and click "Get Signature".

6.) Copy your signature from the left-hand side of the "Signature Generator" page and paste it into "Signature:" box in Gmail.

7.) Scroll to the bottom of the Gmail page and click "Save Changes"

8.) Verify your new signature is working by composing a new message. Your signature should be automatically appended to this new message.

Outlook 2019 Setup outlook :

1.) Start Outlook and navigate to File --> Options --> Mail --> Signatures.

2.) Click "New" to create a new signature and give it any name you prefer. For this example we will use "My Signature".

3.) Type a few nonsense letters and click "Save".

4.) Leave Outlook open and use Windows Explorer to navigate to: C:\Users\YOURUSERNAME\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Signatures (Note that the AppData folder is hidden by default so you may need to select " Show hidden folders ")

5.) Within this folder you will find a file named " My Signature.htm " (or whatever you named your signature). Right-click this file and select "Open with" --> "Notepad".

6.) Fill out the Signature Generator Form above and click "Get Signature".

7.) Copy the HTML on the right-hand side of the Signature Generator Page starting with <table and ending with </table>

8.) Scroll to the very bottom of "My Signature.htm" and look for </body>.

9.) Paste the HTML from the Signature Generator Page one line above </body>.

10.) Look for the <body> tag. If there is anything else written in this tag for instance "<body lang=EN-US link=blue vlink=purple style='tab-interval:.5in'>", delete everything so it simply reads <body>.

11.) Click save and close "My Signature.htm"

12.) Go back to Outlook and double-check your signature is installed properly by composing a new message and selecting your signature from the "Signature" drop down menu.
