In DocuSign, change the domain configuration to "Require all users to authenticate with Identity Provider" (Okta). This change enforces SSO logins and prevents password-based access for everyone but defined exceptions. The exception applies to DocuSign admins as it is best practice to exempt at least two DS admins from the SSO-only requirement.
