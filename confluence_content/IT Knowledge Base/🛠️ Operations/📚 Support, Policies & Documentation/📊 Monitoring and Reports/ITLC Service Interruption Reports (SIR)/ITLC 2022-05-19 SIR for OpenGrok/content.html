Executive summary

A user, Anand Kumar, reported in the IT Hotline that OpenGrok was not working. We restarted the services (httpd and tomcat) and this resolved the issue.

This has been recently become an ongoing issue with OpenGrok with investigation as to the root cause. Other tickets recent tickets were System JIRA ac6def98-2140-30c3-8103-4b52b8b31135 SYSMGR-64110 and System JIRA ac6def98-2140-30c3-8103-4b52b8b31135 SYSMGR-64230 .  Investigation in System JIRA ac6def98-2140-30c3-8103-4b52b8b31135 SYSMGR-62950 and subsequent team discussions indicated doing an upgrade of OpenGrok could solve this, but in the meantime, we'd try a full re-index.
