Privacy Notice

All faxes sent or received through this service are subject to examination by others at Schrodinger in the course of processing them. The faxes also pass through a third-party service provider on the way to their destination. We expect them to be professional and keep our confidences but this expectation isn't absolute. Please consider the sensitivity and privacy of any information you may exchange.

Sending an outbound fax

If you have an electronic document…

  1. Send an email with an attachment to {fax-bot name}+{destination fax number}@ schrodinger.com .

For example, fax-pdx+12125551234@schrodinger.com would send a fax to 1-212-555-1234 .  If the fax number isn't formatted correctly or you forget to attach something an auto-responder will provide advice. The eFax service supports a wide-variety of file formats , but when in doubt PDF is a solid choice.

Every fax must include a country code regardless of area code. Every U.S. fax must start with '1'

An email sent to the Fax bot without a (+) address will generate a help auto-reply.
