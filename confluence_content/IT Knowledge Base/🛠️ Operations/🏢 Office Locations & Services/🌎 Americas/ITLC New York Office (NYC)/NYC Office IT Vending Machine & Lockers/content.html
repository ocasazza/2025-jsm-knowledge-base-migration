The IT vending machine/lockers are located on the 21st Floor right by the Tech Bar.

The vending machine is available for employees to obtain any peripherals that they may need. The IT lockers are used for checking-out or returning any devices (loaner laptops, laptops needing repairs, etc).

How to Use the Vending Machine:

  1. When ready to check-out an item, please scan your Schrodinger ID badge to the badge reader.

  2. Wait for the screen to authenticate; your name will be displayed saying ("Welcome <Name>")

  3. Please select an item number.

  4. To cancel, press the * key.

How to Use the IT Lockers:

  1. Scan your Schrodinger ID badge to the badge reader

  2. Wait for the screen to authenticate; your name will be displayed saying ("Welcome <Name>")

  3. Select the number you would like to check-out/check-in

  4. Press 1 to take, 2 to return to the locker

  5. The selected locker door will then open

  6. IMPORTANT: Please inform IT if you have checked out any of these devices .
