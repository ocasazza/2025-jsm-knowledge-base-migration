Before you begin

The instructions on this page may change as we fine-tune our process for managing license allocation and tracking.

Thank you for your patience if we need to contact you for additional follow-up if our procedures change after you already have installed the software.

If you did not receive or respond to our yearly survey as a ChemDraw user, please contact your manager about license approval.

  • Once managerial approval is granted, please fill out this Google form to capture your intended usage: https://forms.gle/Mq6o7mbBzBXGp7G46 .

  • "Registered" in our context means those who replied to the survey and identified themselves as users of the software, or those who got approved for licenses when we last increased our license count.
