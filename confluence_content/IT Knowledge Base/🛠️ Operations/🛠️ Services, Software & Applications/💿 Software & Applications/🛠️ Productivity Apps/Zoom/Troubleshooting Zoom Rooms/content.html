1. Use either the iPad or the iPort Table to access the meeting screen. Click on the settings icon located in the bottom left corner.

  2. If you encounter audio issues, click on the "Test speakers" option. You will hear a short music clip, allowing you to adjust the volume as necessary.

    Additionally, you can test the microphone. It will prompt you to speak, enabling you to check how well you can hear your voice.

  3. Once in Settings, select "General" and then click the "Reboot" button. If a passcode is required, please contact IT for assistance.

  1. Click the "Reboot" button. The tablets and screen will turn off and will take a few minutes to restart.

  2. Once the tablets are powered back on, ensure that the screen responds to any input from the tablets. After rebooting, the screen should resemble the first screenshot provided above.

If you experience any further issues, please submit a ticket through IT Help on the Okta Dashboard.
