We have enabled non end-user impacting security settings for Zoom to comply with Adaptive Shield recommendations.

Security Settings

  • "Two-Factors Authentication Enforcement" is now turned on . (This should not affect SSO users - everyone with Schrodinger email)
  • "Passwords Containing Consecutive Characters" are not allowed . (This should not affect SSO users - everyone with Schrodinger email)
  • "Weak Passwords Detection" is turned on . (This should not affect SSO users - everyone with Schrodinger email, it will notify users if they have a password defined as "weak".)
  • "Password Includes Special Characters" is turned on to enforce usage of a special character in passwords. (This should not affect SSO users - everyone with Schrodinger email)
  • "Minimum Password Length" is now set to 9 . This is in line with our . (This should not affect SSO users - everyone with Schrodinger email)
  • "Password Protection For Personal Meeting ID (PMI)" is now turned on . (some users had customized this setting but now it's reset to be on by default).
  • "Password Protection For Instant Meetings" is now turned on . (some users had customized this setting but now it's reset to be on by default).
  • "Embed Password In Meeting Link" is now enabled . (It used to be disabled since it included plain text passwords in the link but that is fixed now, this lets you share meeting links that don't require entering passcodes).

Recording Settings

  • Local Recordings is now disabled . You can not have Zoom record your meeting locally. Everything is recorded in the cloud and you can share that via a link with password.
  • Auto Save Chat is now turned off .
  • Live-streaming is now disabled . If you have an approved use case for live-streaming your meeting/webinar please create a Freshservice ticket a few days before your scheduled event.
  • Remote Control is now disabled . You can't let someone else control your screen/mouse while you are sharing your screen.
  • Recording Disclaimer is now enabled . You will see a popup letting you know that the meeting is recorded. If you don't want to be recorded, you have the choice to leave the meeting.
  • Far End Camera Control is now disabled .

User Experience

  • Participants Initial Video Mode is set to off . (You will join all meetings with your videos turned off by default, and you can then get ready and turn on your video if you choose to)
  • Initial Host Video Mode is still on . If you are a host, your video will be turned on by default at the start of the meeting.



As per suggestion from , Slide Control feature has now been enabled .

System JIRA key,summary,type,created,updated,due,assignee,reporter,priority,status,resolution ac6def98-2140-30c3-8103-4b52b8b31135 SYSMGR-55835

  • Secure All Meetings with at least one security method

Original Setting:

  • Require passcode for participants joining by phone was not toggled on.
  • Dialed in users just get admitted if they have the Zoom event info.

New Settings:

  • " Require passcode for participants joining by phone " was turned on at the org level but it's NOT "Locked" . Some users can disable this feature for their own meetings that they host.
    • This setting has now been locked . (04/28/2021, 9:30am PST).
  • A numeric passcode will be required for participants joining by phone if your meeting has a passcode. For meeting with an alphanumeric passcode, a numeric version will be generated.

What prompted the Change?

  • Following a recommendation from and because it makes sense.

Rationale/Justification for Change

  • The setting is intended to further harden the security of our Zoom events and prevent unauthorized attendees from getting into meetings by dialing in without a passcode.
  • The setting can be "Locked" as admin to enforce it once we conclude there are no side effects.
