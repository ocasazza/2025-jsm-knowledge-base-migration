Taking notes is important for everyone at Schrodinger. To ensure notes are backed up and accessible from anywhere, IT recommends and approves the list of Applications below.

Google Keep

Icon:

If you are looking for an app to take notes on the go and see them across all devices, Google Keep is a great option.
Google Keep offers several features for advanced note-taking:

  • Create lists

  • Add collaborators

  • Organize with labels

  • Free-hand write

  • Set Reminders

  • Searchable notes

Ways to access

Google Keep can be accessed by visiting https://keep.google.com from any browser.Â In addition, you can also access Google keep from the Applications Menu from Gmail, or the Google homepage when logged into your Schrodinger account (below Image). The Google Keep app is also available in the Self-Service app on iPad.

Asana

Icon:

Asana is a great way to stay organized with tasks and can be used as an alternative note-taking application. Asana can be downloaded from the web at https://asana.com, or from the the Self Service app on iPad. Once you sign in with your Schrodinger Email account, access is granted.

Features

Asana includes an abundance of features for all types of note-takers!

  • Boards

  • Reporting

  • Goals

  • Forms

  • Timeline

  • Workflow Builder
