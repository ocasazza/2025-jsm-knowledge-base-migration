Licensing

  1. I see a banner that says “View Only. Your account doesn’t allow editing on a Mac. …”

    1. You may not have signed in to your Microsoft account. Please launch any MS Office application. For example: Word , and click Sign In on the initial screen.

    2. You may not have a license assigned to you. Please open a Jira ticket .

  1. Even after signing in to my Microsoft Office account after installation, I still see the “View Only. Your account doesn’t allow editing on a Mac. …” banner. What should I do?

    1. Go to the home screen and make sure you are signed in .

    2. Click Activate as shown as below.

  3. I see a banner that says ‘ Action Required We’ve detected a one-time purchase license. This license can only be used with Office apps downloaded directly from office.com

      1. You will have to activate your account with Schrodinger credentials. Open up any MS Office application (such as Excel). In the menu bar (top left corner of screen), hover over ‘Excel’ and there should be an ‘Activate Office’ option in the drop down menu. From there, it will prompt you to log in and sign in with your Schrodinger email.

  1. What if the above solutions still can’t solve my licensing issue?

    1. On Mac, open your Library folder ( /Users/$USER/Library) and then open Group Containers and move these files to Trash if they are present.

      1. UBF8T346G9.ms

      2. UBF8T346G9.Office

      3. UBF8T346G9.OfficeOsfWebHost

  1. I am receiving this error “Microsoft Excel wants to use your confidential information stored in ….. In your keychain”

    1. This is your keychain for your Mac. Click ‘Allow’ and type in your keychain password. If you have forgotten your password, you can reset your password in ‘Keychain Access’. Doing this will lose all your items currently stored in your previous keychains. In the menu bar, hover over ‘Keychain Access’ and select the 2nd option ‘Preferences’. Then click ‘Reset Default Keychains’ to set new password.

  2. When clicking ‘Activate’, it is prompting me to ‘Choose your Subscription’ and only has purchase options available.

    1. Click ‘Sign in to Purchase’ button and login with your LDAP credentials.

  3. How do I know which license type I have?

    1. Please go to Office Portal and click on “ Subscriptions ” from options.

  4. My MS Office 365 doesn’t have Teams License. How do I get Teams access?

    1. If you are not given a host license for Teams, you will see the following when you launch MS Teams. This is expected, you can freely join any Teams meeting even if you don’t have a Teams license

    3. If you qualify to get a MS Teams license and you need to host meetings with external parties using Teams, then you can request a Teams license using Jira

      1. Please note that the official means of communication is Google Meet for all internal meetings.

  5. I'm getting the message ""Another account from your organization is already signed in on this computer"

    1. See https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/office/troubleshoot/activation/another-account-already-signed-in

  1. How do I know which license is being consumed, I had a perpetual license and it still seems to be active

    1. Please go to https://portal.office.com/account/ and click on Subscriptions . You should normally see “ Microsoft 365 Apps for business”

    2. Alternatively, you can also open an Office app and click on About.

    4. If you have no licenses assigned to you, you’ll see something similar to this. Please request one here. .

  1. I need a license for multiple computers (i.e a Mac, Windows Desktop, and some VMs), do I need multiple licenses?

    1. Unlike the older versions of MS Office, Office 365 licenses are attached to a user not a device. Once licensed, your license is valid for 5 Macs/ 5 Windows, and 5 mobile devices.

    2. You can also log out of a device to log in to a new device to move your license over.

Installation

  1. Where do I download the Office installer for my Mac?

    1. You can go to Microsoft site or to your portal and download the installer.

    2. Allow the auto-updater to install updates automatically.

  3. Do I have to remove my existing MS Office installation for the new one to work?

    1. If your existing Office installation is Office 2016 or later, you don’t need to do that.

    2. [ mass license removal tool will solve this problem ]

  5. Download takes a very long time and fails.

    1. This is most likely an internet connection problem. Please retry the download when nobody else is using your internet for better bandwidth.

  7. Can I just install only Excel instead of the entire suite?

    1. Yes. Please go here if you are on a Mac and pick and choose which apps you want to download & install.

    2. For windows, the answer is NO . However, you can always uninstall what you don’t need after a complete installation

Update

  1. How do I get feature and security updates?

    1. Automatic updates for Office 365 upgrade is enabled by default. To check, open up any Office application such as Word and under ‘Office updates’, check if ‘Updates are automatically downloaded and installed’ is checked.
