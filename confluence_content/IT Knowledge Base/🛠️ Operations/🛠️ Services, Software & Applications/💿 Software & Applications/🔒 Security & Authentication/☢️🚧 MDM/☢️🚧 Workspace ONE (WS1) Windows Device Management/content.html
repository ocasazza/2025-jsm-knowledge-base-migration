Self-Service Portal

The self-service portal lets users see information about and take actions on their enrolled machines. Access to the portal will be granted once

Accessing the Self-Service Portal

  1. Go to https://ds1506.awmdm.com/MyDevice/Login
  2. Enter your Schrodinger email address (usually this is in the form of firstname.lastname@schrodinger.com) and press Next.
  3. If you are already logged into https://schrodinger.okta.com in the same browser, you'll be directed to the self-service portal. Otherwise, you'll be asked to login to your Okta account before being redirected to the self-service portal.

BitLocker recovery key

When your machine is assigned the Workspace ONE policy to enforce BitLocker encryption, it will store the recovery key in your self-service portal.  The policy assigns a new BitLocker recovery key, different from any previous one you may have on record.  To access this new recovery key:

  1. After logging in following the Access instructions, click My Devices, then click the tab for the device of interest.
  2. Click "Go to Details", then MORE, then SECURITY.
  3. Your BitLocker recovery key will be listed under Encryption Recovery Key.
