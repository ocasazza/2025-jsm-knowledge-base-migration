Step 1: Download and Install the Client

Disconnect from the existing VPN to start this process.

Choose the installer appropriate for your system and follow the prompts during installation. No serial numbers or activation codes are necessary.

If you encounter difficulties installing on Linux due to missing dependencies, first try to update your Linux OS ( sudo yum update on CentOS/RHEL/Fedora/AlmaLinux), reboot, and try again.

Optional: Adjust settings for Auto-Login

The Appgate client will have a very long login session. However, you may want the client to automatically sign you in when the session expires. If you would like to turn this on, follow these steps:

  1. Click the kebab menu (three vertical dots) in the upper left (Windows) or right (Mac) of the Appgate window and choose Settings
  2. Scroll down, expand Advanced .
  3. Find the SAML/Certificate auto-sign in toggle and switch it on
