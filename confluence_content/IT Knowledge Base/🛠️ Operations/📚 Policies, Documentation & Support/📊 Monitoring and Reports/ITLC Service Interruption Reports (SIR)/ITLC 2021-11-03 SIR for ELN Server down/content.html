Executive summary

On November 3 we received an email from the ELN vendor Scilligence that they received reports from our CRO users in China that the web site for this app was down. Scilligence investigated and found the IIS (web) service was down and did a service restart correcting the problem.


Background

The Electronic Lab Notebook (ELN) service is used by Schrodinger employees and external contract research organizations to record laboratory data for wet-lab experiments carried out on ligands in pursuit of our drug-discovery efforts.

Incident Description

Users attempting to access the site found it down and reached out directly to the vendor, rather than us, to report the problem and seek a resolution. Scilligence investigated and found the service down and restarted it to restore functionality

Chronology

  • Wednesday, Nov. 3, 2021 7:04 - received an email from Scilligence reporting the problem and the fix

Corrective Action

Scilligence restarted the IIS (web) server

Analysis

No additional analysis was done. At the time of this report writing it's very likely that if any logs existed to shed light here that they have been overwritten in the intervening time. We have failed her to adequately investigate. This lack of analysis will show up in other answers here.

Scilligence didn't provide any details on that and we failed to investigate further (see Analysis section).

Users from the CRO reported the problem to Scilligence.
