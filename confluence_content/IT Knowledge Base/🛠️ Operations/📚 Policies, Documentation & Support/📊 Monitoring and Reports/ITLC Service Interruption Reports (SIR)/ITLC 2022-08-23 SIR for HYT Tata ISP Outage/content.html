Executive summary

Primary ISP in the Hyderabad (TATA) went down due to the physical issue in the ISP medium.

Background

This ISP connection is the primary ISP in the Hyderabad network.

Incident Description

Hyderabad site went down due to an ISP outage. The secondary ISP had to be manually activated to restore connectivity.

Chronology

Incident reported :- 24/AUG/22 12:20 AM IST

Identified as ISP issue :-24/AUG/22 12:30 AM IST

Communicated to the ISP :- 24/AUG/22 12:40 AM IST

Issue resolved from the ISP to bring back the circuit :- 24/AUG/22 3:00AM IST

Total hours of outage is 4 hours.

Corrective Action

This issues are communicated to the ISP for having the high redundancy on the ISP end.

This redundancy is added by the ISP on date 20/Sep/2022 by adding the third link to the existing redundancy on the ISP medium.

And also the secondary ISP link (SIFY) is up and all the tunnels and policies are in place to make the site redundant.
