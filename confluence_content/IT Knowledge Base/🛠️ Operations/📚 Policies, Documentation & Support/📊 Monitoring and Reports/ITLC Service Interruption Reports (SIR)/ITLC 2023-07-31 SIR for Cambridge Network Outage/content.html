Executive summary

Cambridge GTT experienced an unplanned outage on from 12:48pm to 8:15pm. This is considered a partial outage as we still had internet connectivity to certain services. Ex. Cambridge BOS didn't loose connectivity to AWS or GCP. This was primarily because we have redundant tunnels on the BOS firewall.


GTT NMC discovered multiple devices being isolated in Boston and arranged an engineer dispatch to check the devices. Once onsite, the engineer made a console connection to our router and discovered that there was a hardware issue. In order to restore the services the card was reloaded. GTT monitored the device and later confirmed the network was stable.

Background

GTT our primary ISP in the Cambridge office detected isolation of several devices in Boston, US. This caused our Cambridge site to lose partial internet connectivity.
