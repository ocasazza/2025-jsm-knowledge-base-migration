Freshservice is a ticket managing system for support requests. You can submit a new ticket by going to your Okta tile for "IT Help."

Step-by-step guide

You can refer to this guide with screenshots for additional details and instructions.

Below are the basic steps for navigating FreshService:

  • Go to Okta and select the "IT Help" icon. You will automatically be routed to a new page.

  • You will see a search bar, a welcome banner, and two options to select on the landing page.

To search for knowledge articles:

  • Enter key terms in the search bar, such as "gmail" and existing articles will automatically populate the screen.

  • Select the article title that is relevant to your question. You will be routed to a Confluence page with more information. Note: We recommend that you use the search bar function with key words for the quickest results.

To Open a New ticket:

  • If there are no relevant articles, or if you still need help:

    • Select the button labeled "Report an Issue" and you will see a form to complete.

    • Complete the required fields, marked with * . Note: Impact and Urgency will be updated by the Agent working on the ticket.

    • If you want to add others to view the ticket, select the " Add CC " link next to the Requester field. Email addresses will automatically populate as you begin typing.

    • Include all the details of your request or issue, add attachments by selecting the " Attach a file " link below the description field.

    • Click "Submit" once you completed all required fields.

  • You can email "IT-help@" with a new request!

    • The subject of your email will be the subject of your new ticket in FreshService.

    • The body of your email will be the details of the ticket.

    • You can CC anyone else who should watch the ticket.

    • You will see the new ticket in the landing page, the same as a ticket you submit in the UI.

To browse all articles:

  • Select the button labeled "Browse help articles" and you will be taken to the Articles section.

  • Select "General" in the left side panel, and you can "View all 97 articles." You will be routed to the Confluence page of the article you select.

After you open a ticket, you can view all open tickets on the bottom of your landing page. You can sort, filter, and view all of your tickets.
