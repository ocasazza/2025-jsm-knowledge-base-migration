What is FreshService?

Freshservice is a cloud-based IT Help Desk and service management solution that enables organizations to simplify their IT operations. The solution offers features that include a ticketing system, self-service portal and knowledge-base.

How to access FreshService?

FreshService can be accessed through your Okta dashboard. Login to Okta and click on the Freshsevice App icon which has been named “IT Help”.

Creating a Ticket

    1. Log in to Okta and click on the Freshservice App icon.

  1. A landing page will appear. From here you can create a ticket by clicking on “Report an issue”


  2. The other way to open a ticket is from within the FreshService application - Click on the “New” button next to the Search field to access the ticket submission form, then choose “Report an Issue”


  3. On the following screen you are prompted to fill in some fields that explain your issue. Please be as thorough and detailed as possible.

    While you are typing, the right-hand panel will show possible solutions to your issue as well as display links to articles that might provide further information. We encourage you to study that information and apply suggested resolution steps before you submit a ticket.

    When you are done providing details, click the “Submit” button to finish the process of creating a ticket. It will then be placed in our queue and triaged.


Tracking your tickets

After selecting the “View All” link at the bottom of the main portal, you are presented with a list of your tickets. By default, this list shows only your open and pending tickets. Adjust the filter to include resolved or closed tickets or simply show your entire ticket history by selecting the arrow next to “Open or Pending.”

From this list, click on the ticket you wish to view. This will open a detailed view of the ticket hat provides additional information like the name of the agent working on it, the current status and any comments that have been posted on the ticket so far.

From within a ticket, you can post a reply, mark the ticket as closed, or add people to be notified via email about any future activity on the ticket.
