Background information

  • Information on CFR Part 11 Envelope Access and Signatures
  • Box Sign as an alternative - If you already have a Box account, then Box Sign may be an alternative to provisioning a paid DocuSign license for you. Box Sign is best for one-off signature needs or when there is a low volume of documents for signature (5 or less). Box Sign provides the same functionality to standard DocuSign, but is less efficient for large volumes or when there are tracking requirements.
