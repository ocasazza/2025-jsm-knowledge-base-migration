Before Activating


Please note, any directories named 'donotbackup' will not be backed up. If there is content in your home directory you do not wish to be backed up, please move it into a directory named 'donotbackup' before activating and starting your backups.

The backup and restore processes will not prevent your machine from sleeping. See the bottom of the page for instructions on how to prevent your machine from sleeping if you plan to leave your machine unattended to complete a restore or backup.

Preventing your Mac from sleeping


Note: When restoring or backing up -- inSync will not prevent your computer from falling asleep( backups and restores pause while computer is sleeping ), so if you plan to set it and let run unattended you will have to adjust your sleep settings:

  1. Open "System Preferences" from the Apple Menu > System Preferences
  2. In "System Preferences" click Energy Saver .
  3. Select the "Power Adapter" tab.
  4. Check : "Prevent computer from sleeping automatically when the display is off".
  5. Uncheck: "Put hard disks to sleep when possible".

Please remember to set these settings back to normal after your backup or restore completes
