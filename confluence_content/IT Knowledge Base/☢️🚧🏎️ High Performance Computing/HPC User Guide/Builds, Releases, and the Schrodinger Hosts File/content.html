Builds, Releases, and the Schrodinger Hosts File

Various builds of Schrödinger software are installed on the cluster and managed by Build Manager . Older installations will not be installed. If you require an exception, please file a Build Manager JIRA case.

Stable Releases

Stable releases are installed under /nfs/working/releases/ .

Official Builds

Official builds are regularly installed on the new cluster under /nfs/working/builds/OB/ , e.g., /nfs/working/builds/OB/2014-1/build02 .

Nightly Builds

Nightly builds are installed on the clusters under /nfs/working/builds/NB/ .

schrodinger.hosts file

  • PLEASE DO NOT MAINTAIN YOUR OWN HOSTFILE see here for why . If you must, please use http://build-download.schrodinger.com/generatehosts to generate it!

  • The hostfile for the cluster is located at: /nfs/working/builds/install_templates/schrodinger.hosts.<cluster>.base .

For general GPU jobs

  • Use bolt_cpu as -HOST .

For general FEP+ GPU job

  • Use bolt_fepdriver as -HOST .

  • Use the -SUBHOST as shown in the table below.

For a short MD GPU job that completes within 30 minutes

  • Use multisim -HOST <cluster>_short_one_node:1 -mode umbrella … .

  • Do not use -maxjob and -SUBHOST options.
