With only 4 attendees for this session there wasn't a broad or deep enough quorum to tackle the SIR that was ready for review.

In lieu of a formal SIR review we had an informal chat about some of the perceived shortcomings so far in the execution of this board. General topics for that included:

    • Possibility and ramifications of moving to a once a month meeting format
    • Possibility of finding a better time for the meeting that produces less conflict and boosts attendance
    • Increasing the urgency and turnaround time of the reports. Some of them seem to be languishing waiting to be authored
    • Possibility of reformatting our SIR reports to put an emphasis on the solution and next steps by placing them at or near the top of the report.
